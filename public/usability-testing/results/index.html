üìö How to Use This Portal

Step 1: Choose between the Aggregate Dashboard (for all participants) or Individual Reports (for detailed single-participant analysis).

Step 2: Upload the JSON files generated from your usability testing forms. You can drag-and-drop or use the file selector.

Step 3: Explore interactive visualizations, review qualitative feedback, and identify patterns in user behavior.

Step 4: Export your findings as PDF reports, CSV data files, or raw JSON for further analysis.
